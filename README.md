# Church and Dictatorship Archive

A digital archive documenting the role of the Catholic Church during Argentina's last military dictatorship (1976-1983).

## Structure

- `documents/`: Declassified government reports, pastoral letters, academic articles.
- `testimonies/`: Witness testimonies of survivors, families, and church personnel.
- `analysis/`: Curated analyses and research contributions.

## Purpose

This repository serves as a collaborative space for researchers, historians, and human rights advocates to collect, annotate, and analyze primary sources related to ecclesiastical silence, complicity, and resistance during the dictatorship.
